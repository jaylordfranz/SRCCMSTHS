About

Cayetano Science High School is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and nurturing the intellectual growth of its students. With a strong emphasis on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), the school provides a dynamic and challenging environment where young minds can thrive. Our dedicated educators are committed to equipping students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to succeed in a rapidly evolving world. We strive to instill a love of learning, encourage critical thinking, and promote leadership qualities, preparing our students to become future innovators and responsible global citizens.

Welcome Cayetano Science High School

Welcome to Cayetano Science High School, where curiosity meets innovation and academic excellence thrives. Our school is committed to providing a transformative educational experience that empowers students to excel in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). At Cayetano Science High School, we believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where students can explore their passions, develop their talents, and build the skills necessary to succeed in an ever-changing world. With a dedicated team of educators and a focus on holistic development, we prepare our students to become the leaders, thinkers, and problem-solvers of tomorrow.

Cayetano Science High School: Setting the Standard for Excellence

Cayetano Science High School stands as a beacon of academic and personal excellence, setting the standard for education in the region. With a rigorous curriculum focused on STEM education, we challenge our students to reach their full potential and prepare them for the future. Our commitment to high academic standards, character development, and innovation ensures that every student not only excels in their studies but also becomes a responsible and forward-thinking individual. At Cayetano Science High School, we pride ourselves on being the benchmark for quality education, cultivating future leaders who are ready to make a meaningful impact in the world.

Our history

Cayetano Science High School was established with the vision of providing exceptional education in the fields of science and technology. Since its founding, the school has been dedicated to fostering a passion for learning and innovation among its students. Over the years, it has grown into a premier institution known for its academic rigor, outstanding faculty, and commitment to nurturing future leaders in STEM fields. With a rich history of success and a focus on excellence, Cayetano Science High School continues to inspire and empower students to achieve greatness.

2018

In 2018, Cayetano Science High School achieved a significant milestone by being recognized as one of the top performing science high schools in the region, demonstrating exceptional academic performance and innovative programs. The school’s STEM initiatives and student research projects garnered recognition, further establishing its reputation as a leader in scientific education.

2015

The year 2015 marked a remarkable achievement when our students participated in national science fairs and competitions, winning multiple awards and accolades for their groundbreaking research and projects. These accomplishments showcased the talent and dedication of our students, reinforcing the school’s reputation as a hub for future innovators and leaders in STEM fields.

2014

In 2014, our school was honored with the prestigious Excellence in Education Award for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence and its contributions to the advancement of science and technology education. This recognition underscored our efforts in providing students with the tools and resources to excel both inside and outside the classroom.

2012

In 2012, Cayetano Science High School launched a series of community outreach programs aimed at promoting science and technology awareness among local students. These programs not only extended the school's impact beyond its walls but also fostered a spirit of collaboration and knowledge-sharing within the community.
