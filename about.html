Shaping Future Winners!

Celebrating our innovative use of technology to enhance learning experiences and foster academic excellence. This school highlights our commitment to integrating cutting-edge tools and resources into our curriculum, ensuring that students are prepared for the challenges of the digital age.

Empowering Students Through Full Scholarships and Allowances

Cayetano Science High School is dedicated to rewarding academic excellence by offering full scholarships and allowances to deserving students. Through this initiative, we aim to remove financial barriers and provide opportunities for talented individuals to pursue their dreams without worry. Our scholarship program recognizes the hard work, dedication, and potential of our students, supporting them in their journey to academic and personal success. By investing in their future, we continue to foster a culture of excellence, ensuring that every student has the chance to thrive and reach their full potential.

Mission

M

To develop globally competitive students who are academically excellent and morally upright.

Vision

V

We shall emerge as the premier school of science and technology in the National Capital Region with pro-active leadership providing quality technical education to our learners thereby developing their full potentials and producing academically excellent and morally upright individuals.

History

H

With the strong commitment of Congressman ALAN PETER S. CAYETANO to provide the district of Taguig and Pateros (TAPAT) with opportunity to a free and quality science and technology education among the poor and deserving students of public school with the facilities and curriculum that can compete with exclusive private school in Metro Manila, House Bill no. 4448 was introduced on February 21, 2002. It is an act establishing a Science and Technology High School in Pamayanang Diego Silang in Taguig City to be known as the TAGUIG-PATEROS SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY HIGH SCHOOL and the funds were appropriated for its establishment.

About US
