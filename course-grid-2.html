Junior High SchoolThis page is dedicated for the Junior High School information of SRCCMSTHS

The Junior High School division at Cayetano Science High School offers a comprehensive and engaging curriculum designed to build a strong academic foundation. Students are encouraged to explore their interests, develop essential skills, and cultivate a passion for learning. With a focus on academic excellence and personal growth, our Junior High School program prepares students for the challenges of senior high school and beyond, fostering a supportive and stimulating environment that promotes both intellectual and character development.
