Senior High SchoolThis page is dedicated for the Senior High School information of SRCCMSTHS

The Senior High School division at Cayetano Science High School offers an advanced curriculum designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary for success in higher education and beyond. With specialized tracks that cater to various career paths, our program focuses on deepening students' understanding, enhancing critical thinking, and preparing them for the challenges of their chosen fields, while fostering personal growth and leadership.

STEM

The STEM track at Cayetano Science High School is designed for students who are passionate about exploring scientific concepts, technological innovations, engineering principles, and mathematical theories. Students in this track will engage in hands-on projects, research, and real-world applications that prepare them for careers in the rapidly evolving STEM fields. With a focus on critical thinking and problem-solving, the STEM program empowers students to become future innovators and leaders in science and technology.
